Pie

A Brief History of a British Culinary Masterpiece

The Meat Pie. A humble celebration of the culinary skill and mastery of the British chef. Deeply rooted in British culture, the pie is today one the most popular dishes in the UK and a staple part of the British diet, with 75% of the British public eating pie at least once a month. But how did it get here? And how has it become so popular? This is a Brief History of a British Culinary Masterpiece.
